Connecting You With Others


About us

Events Made Easy.

Everest aims to bring a whole new meaning to in-event communication.

From networking events to weddings, our app will bridge the gap between the hosts and guests of an event by vastly improving social interactions among everyone present, as well as increasing organization as a whole.

How it Works

Get your first event up and running within minutes.

Step 1:

Create an event for your organization through the app.

Step 2:

Guests and other hosts can scan your provided QR codes to enter the event.

Step 3:

Guests and hosts interact between themselves and one-another using communication features.

Features

Feature For Hosts For Guests
QR Codes Allow guests to join your events effortlessly through scanning your event's Guest QR Code. Join events without hassle by scanning a unique QR code provided by the event host.
Event Feed Provide real-time, salient updates to all guests and hosts through the in-app event feed. Obtain real-time updates from the host(s) of the event (ie. change in schedule, emergency alerts, announcements).
Instant Messaging Create group chats between hosts and guests to communicate with one another during the event. Discover and chat with other guests with similar interests, as well as with hosts if they seek contact with you.
Unique Profiles Create a unique profile per event to showcase a bit about yourself in the event's context. View profiles of other hosts and guests of your event. Create a unique profile per event to showcase your skills, talents and hobbies. View profiles of hosts and other guests to find common interests and spark connections.
List of Guests and Hosts Gain a high-level understanding of who is attending your event, and keep optimal organization by having a quick reference to all other hosts. Find out what other guests are attending the event, as well as who the hosts are.
